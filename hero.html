My Heroes

One direction is a five-member band made up of Liam Payne, Luis Wolverhampton, Niall Horan, Zayn Malik, and Harry Styles. They all applied as solo candidates for the 2010 edition of the X factor, but the judges put them into a and turned them into groups category. After their first single, What Makes You Beautiful was released in September of 2011, it quickly became the fastest-selling single of the year and peaked at #1 on the UK Singles Chart. They're careers skyrocketed ever since, but as of August 15 2015 the band split. All the members have continued their solo careers.

In their two year existence, One Direction has won a total of 199 awards and has been nominated for 289. Some of these include two consecutive American Music Awards for Artist Of The Year, four BRIT (British Record Industry Trusts Show) Awards, and four World Music Awards. As of this year, all of their hard work has led to the selling of 70 million records worldwide, making them one of the best-selling boy bands of all time.
